How to Compress Images

Our free online image compression tool makes it easy to reduce the file size of your images:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking to browse files
  2. Adjust the quality slider to your preferred compression level
  3. Select your desired output format (JPG, PNG, or WebP)
  4. Click "Compress Image" to process your file
  5. Download the optimized image to your device

Why Compress Images?

Image compression is essential for:

  • Faster website loading - Smaller images load quicker
  • Better SEO rankings - Page speed affects search rankings
  • Reduced bandwidth usage - Save on data transfer costs
  • Improved user experience - Visitors don't wait for images to load
  • Storage optimization - Store more images in less space
